Behavioral Questions 
--------------
 1.⁠ ⁠Tell me about yourself. What are your career goals.







 2.⁠ ⁠Walk me through a project that you delivered successfully.







 3.⁠ ⁠Describe a time you had to resolve a problem.






 4.⁠ ⁠Tell me about a time you disagreed with someone. How did you resolve your disagreement : engineer, product manager, leadership, a customer, or so on. 






 5.⁠ ⁠Tell me about a time you received feedback. How did you handle feedback?






 6.⁠ ⁠Have you ever mentored someone? If so, walk me through how you mentored this engineer. What’s your mentorship style.






 7.⁠ ⁠What role do you usually take in a team?





 8.⁠ ⁠Can you give an example of a time you succesfully delegated?





 9.⁠ ⁠Tell me how you prioritize tasks when everything seems important.






10.⁠ ⁠Have you ever led a team? This can be a subgroup ( of a team ) or some other group of people during a period of time.
